Robertville
The vision

Twenty homes, one neighbourhood.

Robertville is what happens when you take five clusters of four townhomes, point them at a shared courtyard, and design every front door to look at every other front door. The result is an instant block — twenty families who know each other's names by the second week.

Designed by D'Arcy Jones Architects, the homes are West Coast modern in the truest sense: cedar siding, deep eaves, oversized windows, and the warmth of a home that actually belongs to its trees. Half are already spoken for. We'd love to introduce you to the rest.

Inside & out

Every detail considered, none overdone.

A townhome should feel like a house — not a stretched apartment. Every Robertville home is designed for the way families actually live: muddy boots at the door, kids racing up the stairs, the kettle always on.
i.

Private Front Porch

A cedar-clad covered porch with bench seating — wide enough for two adirondacks and a kid's bike helmet hook. Watch the world from the front; live with the neighbours, not from them.

ii.

Mudroom & Boot Bench

A real mudroom — not a glorified hall closet. Built-in bench, six-hook coat rail, four cubbies, and a heated tile floor that actually dries the wet boots that started this whole conversation.

iii.

Family Kitchen

Quartzite counters, panel-ready 36" Fisher & Paykel appliances, and a six-foot island designed for homework, holiday baking, and the third coffee of the morning.

iv.

Backyard & Patio

A fully-fenced private rear yard with covered patio, gas BBQ rough-in, and irrigation pre-installed. Just add the swing set.

v.

Two-Car Side-by-Side Garage

An honest two-car garage — no tandem, no parallel parking acrobatics. Plus EV charger pre-wire and overhead storage for the kayaks, the holiday bins, and the bike fleet.

vi.

Top-Floor Primary Suite

The whole top floor of every home is yours: oversized walk-in closet, ensuite with a freestanding soaker tub, and a private south-facing roof deck for morning coffee.

vii.

The Courtyard

A landscaped shared courtyard at the centre of the development with a community garden, a long picnic table, and a fire-pit ring for late-summer evenings.

viii.

Heat Pump Heating & A/C

Whole-home heat-pump heating and cooling, triple-glazed windows, and a HRV system. Step Code 4 — built for the climate Vancouver actually has, not the one we used to have.

ix.

Belmar 2-5-10 Warranty

The full BC new-home warranty, plus a personal walk-through with our build manager at the 30-day, 6-month, and 1-year marks. We pick up the phone.
