Brinley Estates
The vision

A street that feels like it's always been here.

Brinley Estates is Belmar's most personal project — twenty detached homes on generous lots, set along two quiet streets lined with mature maples that the developer chose to keep rather than clear. Every home faces the sidewalk with a proper front porch, and every backyard backs onto a shared greenway.

The homes are built to lock-up and fully finished — hardwood floors, quartz counters, landscaped yards, and the kind of front door that doesn't need an apology when the in-laws arrive unannounced. Six remain. Each one is ready for immediate occupancy.

Features & finishes

Built the way homes used to be.

Every Brinley home is fully finished and move-in ready. No upgrades to negotiate, no allowances to manage. What you see in the show home is what you get — and what you get is the best we know how to build.
i.

Covered Front Porch

A deep, cedar-lined porch with room for two chairs, a side table, and the morning paper. Every home faces the street — because neighbourhoods are built from the front, not the back.

ii.

Chef's Kitchen

Quartzite counters, a 48" dual-fuel range, panel-ready refrigerator, and an eight-foot island with seating for four. The kitchen Belmar's founder would build for his own family — because he did.

iii.

Main-Floor Great Room

Open-concept living and dining with 10-foot ceilings, wide-plank white oak floors, and a gas fireplace flanked by built-in bookshelves. The room the whole house revolves around.

iv.

Private Backyard

Fully fenced and landscaped with irrigated sod, a covered patio, gas BBQ hookup, and a storage shed. Lot sizes range from 5,200 to 7,400 square feet — room for a trampoline and a garden.

v.

Three-Car Garage

A tandem-plus-single garage with EV charger installed, overhead storage racks, and a workbench nook. Enough room for two cars, the bikes, the stroller, and the holiday bins.

vi.

Primary Suite

The entire upper floor of the five-bedroom plans is a private retreat: oversized walk-in closet, dual-vanity ensuite with freestanding soaker tub, and a private balcony facing the greenway.

vii.

Legal Suite Ready

Every home includes a separate-entry basement suite with its own kitchen, bathroom, and laundry — fully finished and ready to rent, host family, or use as a home office.

viii.

Energy Efficient

Heat pump heating and cooling, triple-glazed windows, HRV ventilation, and Step Code 4 envelope. Built for the climate Vancouver actually has — comfortable year-round, affordable to heat.

ix.

Belmar 2-5-10 Warranty

The full BC new-home warranty, plus personal walk-throughs at 30 days, 6 months, and 1 year. Our build manager's direct number is on every fridge magnet. We still pick up the phone.
