Our story

Three generations of building well.

Belmar started in 1978 with a single duplex in East Vancouver and the conviction that good homes are built the way good furniture is — slowly, by hand, with the next owner already in mind.

Five decades and forty-six communities later, we're still a family business. Our founder's grandchildren walk every site. We answer our own phones. And we still believe that the measure of a builder is not how a home looks the day it sells, but how it feels twenty years on.
